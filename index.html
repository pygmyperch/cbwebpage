About Me

Welcome! I am interested in the conservation, ecology and evolution of aquatic biodiversity, including important species for fisheries. The effects of human development and recent climate change on our natural environment are pervasive and the threat these selective forces pose to global biodiversity is increasing. In my research I use genomics to understand how populations and species evolve in response to their environment.
